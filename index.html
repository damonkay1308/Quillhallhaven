Discover Peace of Mind at Quillhall Haven

Are you looking for a secure home for your loved one?

Quillhall Haven is a peaceful rest home for the aged, situated in a lovely area by the Kloof Golf Course. Our serene garden setting and homely atmosphere create a tranquil environment for our residents. We provide care for assisted living, frail and semi-frail individuals, Dementia/Alzheimer's patients, temporary/holiday stays, and anyone in need of assistance. Experience the comfort and support of our dedicated team in a welcoming community.
